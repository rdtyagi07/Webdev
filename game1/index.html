Hi! Welcome to the Missionaries and Cannibals game

Problem

Solve the famous problem of Cannibals and Missionaries Problem. There are three missionaries and three hungry Cannibals trying to cross a river on a boat. The boat can carry only 2 persons. At any point in time if at either side of the banks there are more Cannibals than Missionaries. Cannibals will eat the missionaries. Now, try and make them cross the river.


Instructions

  • You can move the person to the boat by clicking on it.

  • Click the 'left' or 'right' buttons to move the boat.

  • Reset to restart the game.

  • REMEMBER!! Once anyone get on the boat, it can't get off.
